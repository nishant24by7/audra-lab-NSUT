How It Works

From image upload to actionable forensic verdict in 4 simple steps.

Drop any PNG, JPG, or WEBP. Instantly see 5 forensic canvas views — Thermal, Noise Map, LSB Plane, and ELA — with zero waiting.

Capabilities
Advanced Forensic Intelligence

12 proprietary detection engines running in parallel for the most comprehensive image forensics available.

Logical Physics Audit
Detects impossible real-world inconsistencies — shadow mismatches, reflection anomalies, object merging errors that AI visuals often break.
Biological Anomaly Detection
Scans for non-human facial signatures — unnatural eye-glint patterns, irregular skin pore distribution, absence of micro-vessels in eye whites.
Thermal Heatmap Simulation
Simulates biological heat distribution using jet colormap. AI faces appear thermally flat — real humans have complex blood-flow signatures.
Adversarial Noise Analysis
FFT frequency analysis reveals hidden grid-like AI noise patterns invisible to the naked eye.
Steganography Detection
LSB pixel analysis and trailing data scan to detect hidden malware payloads inside images.
C2PA Provenance Check
Validates digital content authenticity manifests. Missing C2PA metadata on "official" content is an immediate red flag.
Semantic Consistency Check
Verifies if the image "story" makes sense — weather mismatches, impossible locations, contextual inconsistencies.
Social Engineering Intent
Beyond "is it AI?" — determines if it's designed to create panic, impersonate a CEO, or steal credentials.
Batch Redline Reporting
Upload up to 20 images in one enterprise audit with comprehensive per-file security table.
